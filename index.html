iCloud Sync

Snap on your phone. Find it on your Mac.

Everything you capture on iPhone or iPad appears instantly in SnapBrief on your Mac — and vice versa. Start a brief on your phone in a meeting, expand it at your desk.

Sync uses your private iCloud container. SnapBrief never touches your data.
